About Mopen's Liquid Soap

Mopen's Liquid Soap is proudly owned by a young Cameroonian entrepreneur based in Bafoussam, Cameroon. Our mission is to provide high-quality liquid soap that is affordable, effective, and safe for everyday use.

Over the years, our products have gained the trust of many customers because of their excellent cleaning power, pleasant fragrance, and long-lasting quality.
